About IServe Facilities

Family-owned and operated since 1993. Built on a dream, a van, and a commitment to doing things right.

Our Story

IServe Facilities started in 1993 with a simple dream: deliver professional, reliable facility services that businesses in Central Kansas could truly count on. What began with a single van and a whole lot of hard work has grown into one of the region's most trusted commercial cleaning companies.

Over 33 years later, the values from day one have never been forgotten. We still believe that doing right by our clients — showing up consistently, communicating proactively, and going beyond what's expected — is the foundation of everything we do.

Today, our team of 100+ employees brings a combined 135 years of experience to facilities across Wichita, El Dorado, Hutchinson, and the surrounding communities.
