About IServe Facilities

We started with a simple belief: facilities management done right changes the way people feel about the spaces they work in.

Our Story

Built on a Belief That
Standards Matter

IServe Facilities was founded in 2010 by a team of facilities management professionals who had seen the same story play out too many times: cleaning contractors who looked great on paper but delivered inconsistent, unreliable service the moment they were hired.

The problem, we believed, was training. Most cleaning companies hired staff and sent them straight to a site with minimal preparation. The result was predictable — varying quality, high turnover, and frustrated clients.

Our answer was CleanCheck — a rigorous, structured training and certification system that every IServe team member completes before their first deployment. It transformed how we operate and became the foundation of our reputation for reliability and quality.

Today, IServe serves over 500 clients across commercial, healthcare, education, and industrial sectors, with a team of 1,200+ CleanCheck-certified professionals who take pride in their work every single day.
